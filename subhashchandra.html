SubhashChandra Bose Program

For the preparation of competitive exams, the college has tied up with lbs academy and made it an academic partner, in which career guidance and free classes are provided to the students regularly for the preparation of competitive exams. Along with this, on the recruitment of various government jobs from time to time, lbs academy organizes classes for the preparation of competitive examinations at the lowest fee in the college premises itself. For the preparation of UPPSC, MPPSC, CAT, MAT, SSC, Bank, RAILWAY, and other competitive exams, one, two, three year courses respectively have been prepared in such a way that students can prepare for competitive exams during college education only.

  • 3 year UPSC/MPPSC Programme By LBS Academy
  • Short Team Programmes By LBS Academy
  • Free Competitive Classes For College Students
