National Service Scheme

The National service Scheme(NSS) was started by the Goverment of India on September 24,1969,in the birth centenary year of the Father of the Nation,MahatGandhi,with the intention of inculcating social responsibility, consciousness,self-motivated discipline and respect for labor in the students associated with higher education.

Greeting:Nss members shout "Jai Hind" to greet each other.

The motto:The motto of NSS is "Mai Nahi AAp(Not Me But You ) This motto sums up the essence of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam,which stands for selfless service."

Objective:The objective of NSS is "Development of personality of the students through social service"
